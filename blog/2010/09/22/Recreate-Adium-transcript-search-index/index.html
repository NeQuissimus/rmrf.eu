Recreate Adium Transcript Search Index

22.Sep 2010 | Comments

Last week I wanted to look for something in my Adium logs (they call them transcripts). I even knew who had sent me the advice, so I selected the person from my contacts and started typing into the search box. What happened? Absolutely nothing!

I found that my search index had been corrupted. But where do you find Adium’s transcript search index? Doing this in Terminal helped me and Adium immediately began creating a new index:

rm ~/Library/Caches/Adium/Default/*

This deletes your old index (and cached images) which Adium immediately recognizes. After about three seconds (9 MB in 572 log files for me) Adium will have created a new index which should work smoothly.

Edit:
The guys from Adium just sent me this wiki article: http://trac.adium.im/wiki/BrokenTranscriptSearch It describes the exact files that will need to be deleted in Adium 1.3 (I just threw out the entire contents of the folder – e.g. avatars would have to be reloaded) and a command that takes care of this process in 1.4 and later.
